About.

Boasting an innovative vision that aims at elevating F&B standards across Egypt, SAAL Invest crafts multi-sensory experiences across varied food categories, all steeped in superior quality, consistent service, food safety, as well as value for money to guarantee the highest level of customer satisfaction at all times. With over 20 years of expertise in the food business, as well as more than 50 leading restaurants in both Egypt and the USA, SAAL Invest combines technical know-how, flavorsome recipes and unique cooking techniques to satisfy the ever-evolving needs of contemporary market segments.

Building Brands of Compelling ValueFounded in 2003, SAAL Invest is an Egyptian food and beverage company, specializing in casual dining concepts of compelling value. With an eye on excellence and a commitment to exceed customers’ every expectation, we focus on introducing decent & valuable dining experiences through powerful brands that put the concept of ‘food with integrity’ at heart. As a family run business, we care to always offer mouth-watering recipes using the freshest ingredients.

Our Philosophy

  • • Building powerful brand concepts of compelling value through a distinctive formula that combines effective marketing strategies and breakthrough product innovation based on meticulous research to constantly fulfill market gaps with innovative food brands of utmost excellence.
  • • A passion for trending ideas by keeping a close eye on rising food trends both locally and globally to meet market demands with fresh and out of the box food concepts that appeal to a wide target segment.
  • • Quality in every bite by sourcing the freshest ingredients and quality recipes while implementing the highest standards of quality control and food safety across all operations to guarantee a tasty experience with a high nutritional value, that cannot be duplicated elsewhere.
  • • Creating industry-leading returns through attractive franchising opportunities and disciplined use of capital aimed at maximizing long-term shareholder value.
  • • Investing in our workforce through on-going training, encouragement of fresh ideas and employee development, believing that each member can truly make a difference in the workplace.

Our Values

FOOD WITH INTEGRITY

SAAL Invest is synonymous with unmatched quality, variety and affordability. By putting the concept of ‘food with integrity’ at the forefront of all our operations, we ensure that nothing but fresh and good ingredients set the core foundation of our brands and menus alike. We guarantee a 100% safe food experience crowned by irresistible taste and exceptional nutritional value at all times.

HAPPY CUSTOMERS - SOUND INVESTMENT

At SAAL Invest, we understand that the key to sound investment lies in brands with an ongoing stream of happy customers. To achieve that, we focus on our employees’ satisfaction first by investing in technical training and benefit packages to nurture positive employee growth which in return results in enhanced customer relationships, as well as increased sales.

INSPIRING INNOVATION

Innovation lies at the heart of SAAL Invest’s business being an instrumental tool to exponential growth and client satisfaction. Whether in recipes, offerings or in-store experiences, we are always keen on introducing breakthrough ideas that adapt to the market’s ever-evolving needs while staying ahead of the game with creative casual dining concepts.

EVER EVOLVING

We understand that the key to grow and survive in an ever-changing industry is to be able to constantly evolve to meet customers’ diverse needs. Therefore, at SAAL Invest, we keep an eye on fresh food trends, knowing that there is always room for improvement and that’s how we develop and lead.

TECHNOLOGY BASED

Believing in technology and the digital transformation are the main elements for any business to grow. We are not only a trend setter for QSRs’s food innovation, but we also focus on applying all types of technology in our operation starting from the online food ordering, aggregator partnerships, in-store self- ordering and chatbot for home delivery.
